Group Reflection

Team contribution

Overall our group has come a long way and we are all pleased to be in our group. We all get a long well and have open communication with each other. Our team has a good mix of personalities All with minimum IT experience although we are enthusiastic and determined to work together combine our efforts.

In the beginning we were all concerned with the communication, whether it was concerns of missing Team members, or being the missing team member working out how to get into contact with the team. In the big picture we all just wanted to connect and start communications and contributions.

Our weekly meetings went really well, we openly discussed ideas, structures. and questions. The weekly meetings were very informative and were the roots of our structure. We all found comfort and understanding in the weekly meetings as we felt comfortable bringing up a topic and have general discussions about a2.

As we all come from different industries and have different commitments, we believe we should have discussed our standard routines, so we knew who to contact & when should we have had questions. We should of all stated days and times we will roughly be online, that would of allowed a more refined support network, with less times waiting on replies.

Each team member had a different experience within the group and personally learned different aspects. One thing we all learned together is how important the initial communication is. Its important to get through the initial steps of understanding how we will communicate to each other and even the rough guidelines on future meetings. once that initial introduction hurdle is overcome the communication evolves a lot faster and every team member feels more comfortable opening up and discussing topics. The more meetings and discussions we can have, the more everyone feels comfortable with the team members, and opening the door to conversations they may not necessarily open without the strong team environment and communication. Although we are still somewhat hesitant shinning the light on our potential flaws, understanding our team members openness and willingness to help is beneficial. This only comes with time and as we all work and grow together we are able to each shine a brighter light on our own learning insecurities and work through them as a team. The A Team have overcome hurdles such social anxieties, learning insecurities and confusions. We All contributed greatly and were open to discussions. Overall we are happy to be in a team together.
