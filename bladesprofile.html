Born in QLD Australia, I am Irish-Scottish on my father’s side and Japanese. I am interested in world history and events and like to study different cultures. I also have two pet cats, Anchovy and Ravioli.

I have always had a passive interest in IT from a young age, as I was surrounded by it for as long as I can remember. My mother and father were very early adopters of computers in the ‘80s and even now I can still remember, as a child, my mother using a computer and showing me the amazing things it could do. I have always loved computers and technology and when I was a teenager, I came across a video about programming on YouTube. In the video the programmer gave a quick backstory on himself and how he got into IT. I immediately related to his story and it stimulated my interest. I then began watching video after video on programming. Finding the magic and wonder for something new, and since then I knew my future would be in IT . IT experience is limited to self learning videos mostly from youtube, no official IT experience or studies as such.

Ideal Jobs

Software Tester

Blades Ideal Job is structured and formulaic as a Software Tester. Critical thinking and problem solving is key, Blades roll will require thorough testing of every aspect of the software and quality control and bug testing. Blade would assess the software, gather data, set up tests, trouble shoot and report on the errors and possible solutions he finds. In some scenarios he may assist repairing the issues so software development skills are required, ideally a computer science degree would assist this position. Blade would program code within established testing protocols, guidelines, and quality standards to ensure systems perform to specification.
