Machine Learning

What does it do?

Machine learning at its core is essentially a computer that fetches and processes data, and then makes decisions and predictions accordingly based on the data it has retrieved. The computer can process information millions of times faster than humans when it comes to viewing and collecting that data. Some of the basic functions that machine learning can do:

  • Interprets internet search algorithms.
  • Detects what type of emails are spam in your inbox.
  • Sorts out and compiles different categories of information on data bases.

More advance forms of machine learning have come to be known as Artificial Intelligence or AI, which can complete much more in depth tasks by following a specific program which allows the computer to learn and make choices without any supervision required. This is only a partial or narrow form of AI. Narrow/weak AI is only intelligent at performing certain tasks. This type of AI and machine learning follows a specific algorithm to complete its function.

Computer programmers can design and create these algorithms that the computers can then process the data and give accurate calculations such as 'how much the steering wheel of the car needs to be pulled when making a turn on the road' or separating and classifying different types of animal species. AI's can also learn and improve over time. They can grow their understanding and evolve, doing specific tasks much faster than any human can. Reinforcement learning is a type of learning that an AI uses to go through the trials and errors of experience. It finds out what worked and what didn't work and then adjusts appropriately to locate the best possible outcome or solution. For example, an AI learning the games Chess or Go, is able to play itself millions of times to work out the best moves and strategies in order to win.

What can be done now?

With all the progress and advancements in Technology, no human level of AI has been demonstrated yet and some people believe its not even possible. However, the use of Narrow AI is already being used throughout businesses and services to successfully save time, money and overall efficiency. Many of these innovations have come from AI being programmed to follow a certain algorithm and apply the use of Deep Nural Net. These types of AI can:

  • Find people's faces in photos and videos
  • Drive vehicles autonomously
  • Translate human speech
  • Diagnose medical conditions
  • Create Virtual assistants

What's coming next?

IT has been making people's jobs obsolete throughout history. Machine learning and AI will continue to be no different. Within just a few years (estimated around 2024) it's likely that AI will take over duties of writing minor coding in the near future. It will also be able to run faster and more improved algorithms that do not need to be monitored and will eventually be able to write entire codes of markup and styling languages (HTML & CSS).

Technologies and other developments that make this possible?

The continuous upgrades to computer processors and systems year by year contributes to further improvement and capabilities of what machine learning and AI can do. The constant involvement from programmers and engineers also helps, as they come up with new and inventive ideas to continuously increase the capacity of an ever-growing conglomerate of information and discovery.
