Ideal Jobs

Media & Tech Lead - Computer Systems Engineer/Architect.

Martina’s ideal job is a Media and Tech Lead - Computer Systems Engineer/Architect. This role is to gain an understanding of current computer systems and how they could improve either from errors, time efficiencies, design, and reliability.
