Ideal Jobs

Peter’s ideal job Business Analyst, IT.

Peter’s role is as a creative and critical thinker. Peter needs to have strong communication and understanding of business requirements, attention to detail, and analytical thinking. Research and assessing current processes, technologies, and systems will give Peter a thorough understanding of problems and challenges to overcome. Peter will create data models, process diagrams, and wireframes. Peter will also need to manage relations while working closely with business decision-makers and development and testing teams to offer guidance and advice on the best scenario solutions to fit the business needs. Peter will need the tech lingo and knowledge to liaise with developers and systems architects to ensure the system is correctly implemented. He will monitor solutions and continuous improvement. Peter will also need to train the client's staff, producing manuals and documents of functionality. Generally, a Bachelor's degree in business administration, information systems, or a related field is ideal, and Knowledge of the International Institute of Business Analysis would be a plus.
