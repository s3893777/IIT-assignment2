I have always loved computers and technology and when I was a teenager, I came across a video about programming on YouTube. In the video the programmer gave a quick backstory on himself and how he got into IT. I immediately related to his story and it stimulated my interest. I then began watching video after video on programming. Finding the magic and wonder for something new, and since then I knew my future would be in IT.
